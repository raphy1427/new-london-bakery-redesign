# Royal Touch Barber Redesign Proposal

## 1. BUSINESS PROFILE
- Name: Royal Touch Barber
- Category: Barber shop
- Location: 140 W Main Street, Somerville, New Jersey 08876
- Website: https://royaltouchbarberllc.com/

## 2. CURRENT WEBSITE PROBLEMS
- Design issues: excessive repeated sections, template-heavy layout, inconsistent capitalization, visible copy errors, and weak content editing.
- UX/navigation issues: too much duplicated content, cluttered service and pricing presentation, and weak prioritization of booking actions.
- Mobile issues: repetitive sections create scroll fatigue, section order is inefficient, and the content hierarchy is weak for quick phone users.
- Trust/credibility issues: placeholder testimonial references to `TrimHub Barbershop`, spelling issues such as `Hair & Bread Stytle`, and stat blocks that do not feel validated.
- Missing features: clearer booking flow, better team proof, real review integration, stronger local SEO structure, and a more organized haircut gallery.

## 3. REDESIGN STRATEGY
- Design style: bold/high-contrast
- Layout philosophy: multi-section landing page with booking-first information hierarchy
- Suggested structure:
- Hero section
- About business
- Services and pricing
- Barbers / team
- Reviews / social proof
- Location + map
- Contact CTA

## 4. FEATURE UPGRADE LIST
- online booking integration
- contact form
- WhatsApp / call button
- haircut gallery with cleaner filtering
- reviews integration from Google
- SEO improvements for Somerville barber searches
- mobile-first redesign

## 5. CONCRETE FIX LIST (ACTIONABLE)
- remove duplicated sections across home and services pages
- replace placeholder testimonials with verified real customer reviews
- fix spelling, grammar, and inconsistent capitalization site-wide
- simplify the service menu into clear haircut, beard, kids, and color categories
- rebuild the homepage around one strong hero and one booking CTA
- redesign the pricing section into a compact scannable layout
- restructure team profiles with real photos, specialties, and booking links
- rebuild mobile spacing and section order to reduce scroll fatigue
- modernize the color palette and typography for stronger premium positioning
- add local trust signals such as hours, map, review rating, and service guarantees

## 6. BUSINESS VALUE ANGLE
- improved customer trust by removing placeholder content and visible quality issues
- higher conversion rate through a clearer booking path
- better mobile usability for users searching quickly on their phones
- stronger local competition advantage by presenting the shop as established and credible rather than template-based
