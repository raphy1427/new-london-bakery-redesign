# New London Bakery Redesign Proposal

## 1. BUSINESS PROFILE
- Name: New London Bakery
- Category: Bakery / wholesale bread supplier
- Location: 92 Truman Street, New London, Connecticut 06320
- Website: https://newlondonbakery.com/

## 2. CURRENT WEBSITE PROBLEMS
- Design issues: repeated homepage sections, dated GoDaddy template feel, weak typography hierarchy, inconsistent spacing, and low visual polish.
- UX/navigation issues: limited navigation, shallow product information, repeated homepage content, and no strong separation between retail and wholesale intent.
- Mobile issues: dense text blocks, weak visual hierarchy, and a scanning experience that likely feels cramped on smaller screens.
- Trust/credibility issues: visible typo (`No Presevatives used`), outdated copyright year, minimal business story, and weak proof of freshness, process, and client trust.
- Missing features: no structured wholesale inquiry flow, no clear CTA system, no strong product gallery presentation, and no review/social proof integration.
- Image issues: several product and gallery-related images appear soft or compressed, which lowers perceived product quality.

## 3. REDESIGN STRATEGY
- Design style: friendly/local community style
- Layout philosophy: multi-section landing page with wholesale-first information hierarchy
- Suggested structure:
- Hero section
- About business
- Bread categories and product highlights
- Wholesale service area and delivery details
- Reviews / social proof
- Location + map
- Contact CTA

## 4. FEATURE UPGRADE LIST
- wholesale order / inquiry form
- contact form with business-type selector
- call button for fast ordering
- product gallery with clearer categories
- reviews integration
- SEO improvements for local bakery and wholesale bread searches
- mobile-first redesign

## 5. CONCRETE FIX LIST (ACTIONABLE)
- replace the repeated homepage hero with one clear intro and CTA
- rebuild navigation around `Home`, `Products`, `Wholesale Orders`, `About`, and `Contact`
- redesign the typography system for stronger headings and easier scanning
- rewrite copy to remove typos and tighten product descriptions
- reorganize products into clear categories with consistent product cards
- rebuild the mobile layout with cleaner spacing and larger tap targets
- improve gallery presentation with better cropping consistency and captions
- enhance soft or compressed gallery/product images without changing their dimensions
- add trust elements such as years in business, delivery coverage, and client types served
- add primary CTA buttons for `Call to Order` and `Request Wholesale Info`

## 6. BUSINESS VALUE ANGLE
- improved customer trust through cleaner presentation and fewer visible quality errors
- higher conversion rate on calls and inquiries by making ordering paths obvious
- better mobile usability for restaurant owners and local buyers browsing on phones
- stronger local competition advantage through a more professional wholesale-focused presentation
